How to Use ExplainerAI

1

Add Scenes

Click "Add Scene" to create scenes. Each scene is one segment of your video.

2

Write & Voiceover

Enter narration text, pick a voice, and click "Generate Audio". Adjust volume or mute per scene.

3

Add Visuals

Type an image prompt to generate AI art, or upload your own image/video.

4

Customize Effects

Choose an animation (zoom, pan, Ken Burns) and transition (crossfade, slide, wipe) for each scene.

5

Render & Download

Set your orientation (landscape/portrait), then click "Render Video" to produce a final MP4.
